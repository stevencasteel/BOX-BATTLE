1. HARMONIC ROOT AND MODE SELECTION
-----------------------------------
The background synth sequences reside strictly in C Natural Minor (C Aeolian)
and periodically pivot through C Harmonic Minor (introducing the B-natural major 
dominant turnaround). 

To ensure combat sound effects slice cleanly through the dense lower-register 
bass synthesizer frequencies, the auditory accretion is mapped to the C Dorian 
Mode:
  Scale: C, D, Eb, F, G, A, Bb (1, 2, b3, 4, 5, 6, b7)

The major 6th scale degree (A-natural, 1.6818 ratio) provides a bright, 
heroic, "space-knight" acoustic tone that prevents harmonic mud and gives 
satisfying auditory feedback upon skill execution.


2. EQUAL TEMPERAMENT FREQUENCY SCALING
--------------------------------------
To calculate in-key pitches dynamically for sfx, we utilize standard 12-Tone 
Equal Temperament (12-TET) mathematical multipliers:
  Multiplier = 2^(n/12)

The C Dorian Mode multiplier ratios are:
  C   (Unison):  1.0000
  D   (Maj 2nd): 1.1225
  Eb  (Min 3rd): 1.1892
  F   (Perf 4):  1.3348
  G   (Perf 5):  1.4983
  A   (Maj 6th): 1.6818
  Bb  (Min 7th): 1.7818
  C   (Octave):  2.0000


3. SYSTEM ACCRETION INTEGRATION
-------------------------------
3.1 State Tracking:
    The Zustand gameplay store ('src/store/useGameStore.ts') maintains an 
    active combo tracker ('comboCounter: number').

3.2 Accumulation Rules:
    - Increments with each successful hit (BOSS_HURT, MINION_HURT).
    - Resets immediately back to zero when the player lands on the floor 
      (PLAYER_LANDED) or receives contact damage (PLAYER_HURT).

3.3 SFX Chaining:
    - Hit confirm strikes in 'BossSFX.ts' are grounded to C5 (523.25 Hz) and 
      pitch up in 12-TET equal intervals, looping with octave multiplications
      if the combo exceeds 12.
    - Slashes and downward pogo bounces in 'PlayerSFX.ts' scale their base 
      oscillators relative to the active scale multiplier.
