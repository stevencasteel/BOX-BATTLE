===========================================================================
  BOX BATTLE WEB CABINET // DESIGN EXCLUSIONS REGISTER
  Official Out-of-Scope & Non-Goal Specifications
===========================================================================

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
This document registers design directions, mechanics, and camera behaviors 
that are explicitly excluded from the Box Battle web engine. Keeping these 
features out of the project preserves the classic structural framing of a 
physical arcade cabinet.

2. SPECIFIC EXCLUSIONS
----------------------

2.1  EXCLUDED: Dynamic Camera Panning (Auto-Framing)
     * Description: The camera must never dynamically pan, scroll, or shift 
       its offset coordinates to follow the player, boss, or average 
       midpoints.
     * Reason: The game is played inside a fixed-aspect retro CRT monitor 
       simulated on a single viewport screen. Viewport scrolling degrades 
       the physical arcade cabinet illusion and makes spatial awareness 
       unpredictable on high-speed dashes.

2.2  EXCLUDED: Dynamic Auto-Zooming
     * Description: The viewport scale matrix must remain flat and static 
       at 1.0 (or its container-defined base scale). The camera must never 
       zoom in or out based on proximity between combatants.
     * Reason: Scale alterations introduce pixel-stretching artifacts on a 
       pure HTML5 Canvas. A fixed scale preserves consistent hit-box 
       proportions and keeps the screen clean and legible.

3. MAINTENANCE OF DESIGN BOUNDS
--------------------------------
Any future layout adjustments must honor these exclusions. The screen area 
will remain fully visible at all times, with visual movement limited strictly 
to screenshakes (cam-tremors) during high-impact events (such as damage, 
dashes, or boss phase changes).
